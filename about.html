What We Do

MACRO ACRES LIMITED is a fully indigenous consultancy firm incorporated in May 2011 and has since been offering dedicated services in the core areas of our businesses.

We are constantly evolving total solutions to deliver projects in Architectural and Allied Consultancy Services in line with international standards.

MACRO ACRES LIMITED undertakes all categories of Building and Engineering Design from conception to handover and has since evolved to be a sought-after Developer with the mission of delivering VALUE ADDED HOMES.
