What is MACRO ACRES LIMITED's main area of expertise?

MACRO ACRES LIMITED specializes in architectural consultancy, offering a comprehensive suite of services that range from feasibility studies to design, supervision, and project management. Our approach combines indigenous expertise with international standards, ensuring top-tier solutions for our clients..

+
-
Since when has MACRO ACRES LIMITED been in operation?

MACRO ACRES LIMITED was incorporated in May 2011. Since then, we've been dedicatedly serving our clients, continuously evolving to offer holistic architectural solutions.

-
Does MACRO ACRES LIMITED provide feasibility studies before starting a project?

Yes, we do. Our feasibility studies provide prospective clients with pre-design insights based on in-depth professional analyses. This helps evaluate the potential value of a project, offering a glimpse into cost estimates and the expected return on investment.

+
-
Is MACRO ACRES LIMITED involved only in the design phase or do they oversee the entire project lifecycle?

MACRO ACRES LIMITED is involved in the entire project lifecycle. Beyond design, we offer post-contract supervision to ensure that the final outcome aligns perfectly with the proposed design. Additionally, our project management services span both pre and post-contract stages, ensuring optimal allocation of resources to achieve business objectives.

What sets MACRO ACRES LIMITED apart from other architectural firms?

Our strength lies in our deep understanding of our clients' diverse needs, allowing us to propose prime solutions that guarantee real value for money. Our commitment to excellence, combined with our adherence to international standards and indigenous expertise, ensures each project we undertake is a testament to our dedication and proficiency.
