Arthur is the MD/CEO of Central Contractors Limited. His training as an Architect has accorded him a vantage position of Designing and Supervising various projects ranging from single unit buildings to multifarious projects. He has a passion to translate good designs into reality by utilizing an integrated approach that is technology driven for the best Architectural and Engineering solutions.

He is also the founder and Principal Partner of a leading Architectural Firm; Macro Acres Limited. Before then, he worked with Tunji Kusa and Partners, Architectural and Building Consultants and FROMEB Associates. He has equally served as Consultant to Landbase Associates Nigeria Limited and SKEEMS Limited where he was involved in projects for Central Bank of Nigeria, National Programme on Immunization, University of Jos Teaching Hospital and National Primary Healthcare Development Agency.

Arthur is an alumnus of University of Jos, where he holds a Bachelor and Masters Degree in Architecture. He is a full member of Architects Registration Council of Nigeria (ARCON) and Nigerian Institute of Architects (NIA). He is married with kids.
